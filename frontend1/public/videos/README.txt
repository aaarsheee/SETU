- basic-greetings.mp4
- numbers-1-10.mp4
- family-members.mp4
- common-verbs.mp4
- question-words.mp4
- time-and-dates.mp4
- emotions-feelings.mp4
- complex-sentences.mp4
- professional-vocabulary.mp4

Supported formats: MP4, WebM, MOV, AVI
Recommended: MP4 for best browser compatibility

After adding your videos, update the lesson data in LearnPage.tsx with the correct video paths.
